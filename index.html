Why Satyalok?

'Satyalok' literally means "The truthful divine place to live" or "A place where truthful divine people live". If we are to declare our group/organization/website motto in one sentence, we can conclude, "let's make this world a little more better place to live". We believe - "It's never too late to start a good step towards goodness".
